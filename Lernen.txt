There exists a $\mathbb{P}$-a.s. unique $\hat{Y} \in K$, s.t. <br>\begin{enumerate}[label=(\roman*)]<br>&nbsp; &nbsp; \item $\left\| Y - \hat{Y} \right\| = d(Y, K) = \inf \{\left\| Y - X\right\|_2 : X \in K\}$<br>&nbsp; &nbsp; \item $Y - \hat{Y} \perp X$ for all $X \in K$, i.e. $Y - \hat{Y} \in K^\perp$ <br>\end{enumerate}<br>$\hat{Y}$ is called the orthogonal projection of $Y$ onto $K$.
